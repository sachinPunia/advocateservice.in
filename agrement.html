Agreements

Posted 7 September 2021 by Advocate Monika Poonia

An agreement is a formal decision about future action which is made by two or more countries, groups, or people. Agreement on something is a joint decision that a particular course of action should be taken.

An Agreement is a promise between two entities creating mutual obligations by law. Section 2(e) of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 defines an agreement as ‘Every promise and every set of promises, forming the consideration for each other, is an agreement’.

Types of agreements:-
  • Memorandum of Understanding.
  • Power of Attorney /li>
  • Sale deed, Agreement for sale
  • Gift deed
  • Leave and License
  • Affidavit
  • Marriage Certificate
  • employer and employee agreement etc.
